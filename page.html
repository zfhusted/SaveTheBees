SAVE THE BEES


HOW TO PLAY

  • Press the up (↑) arrow to go up.

  • Collect the pollen.

  • Press the spacebar to deliver it to the flowers to earn points!

HOW TO PLAY

  • Don't drop the pollen in the poisoned flowers or you'll lose points!

  • Don't let the bee fly too low or you'll lose a life!

  • Don't get poisoned by the insecticide or you'll lose a life!

ABOUT US

Save the Bees was created by Zoe Husted, Jade Jordan. and Elena Murphy as their final project for the Girls Who Code summer immersion program hosted by Expedia in 2015.

The game was made to raise awareness of the declining bee population and the devastating effects that will result from the extinction of bees.
